Caring for Body, Mind, and Spirit

The Pastoral Care Ministry is committed to walking with parishioners through lifeâ€™s milestones and struggles. Through prayer, presence, and service, our team of clergy, lay ministers, and volunteers provides guidance, comfort, and spiritual nourishment.

  • Hospital & Home Visits: Bringing Holy Communion, prayers, and encouragement to the sick and homebound.
  • Bereavement Support: Offering comfort, funeral planning assistance, and ongoing care for grieving families.
  • Spiritual Counseling: Providing one-on-one guidance and prayer for those facing personal or family challenges.
  • Prayer Ministry: Lifting up parish needs and intentions through regular prayer groups and intercession.
  • Sacramental Support: Preparing individuals to receive the Sacraments in times of illness or special need.

No one in our parish should feel alone in their journey of faith. Whether you are experiencing illness, loss, or simply need a listening ear, the Pastoral Care team is here to help.

To request a visit or learn more about volunteering, please contact the parish office or speak with a priest after Mass.
