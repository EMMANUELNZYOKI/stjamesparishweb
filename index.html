About St. James Catholic Church

Rooted in the Gospel of Jesus Christ, St. James Catholic Church is a welcoming parish family dedicated to prayer, service, and spiritual growth. We strive to nurture a vibrant community where people of all ages can encounter Christ through the celebration of the sacraments, inspiring liturgy, and lifelong faith formation. Guided by the Holy Spirit, we seek to live the values of the Gospel by fostering compassion, offering outreach to those in need, and building friendships grounded in love and respect. Together, we embrace our call to discipleship—sharing the Good News, supporting one another in life’s joys and challenges, and working for peace and justice in our local community and beyond.

  • Celebrate the Holy Mass and sacraments as the heart of our faith.
  • Offer ministries and outreach programs that serve those in need and build community.
  • Provide opportunities for faith formation for children, youth, and adults, nurturing lifelong discipleship.
  • Encourage active participation in parish life through prayer groups, choirs, and service ministries.
  • Promote a spirit of hospitality where all feel welcomed, valued, and loved as members of God’s family.
  • Support families in living their Catholic faith at home and in the wider community.
  • Foster a deep commitment to social justice, peace, and care for God’s creation.
  • Provide spiritual guidance and pastoral care to those facing illness, grief, or life’s challenges.
  • Inspire missionary discipleship, encouraging all to share the Good News of Jesus Christ in daily life.

Guided by the example of St. James the Apostle, we strive to love God above all things and to share His mercy and hope with the world. Whether you are new to the Catholic faith or have been a parishioner for many years, there is a place for you in our church family. We welcome seekers, lifelong Catholics, and those returning to the faith with open hearts and joyful hospitality. Together, we grow in prayer, celebrate the sacraments, and support one another through life’s joys and challenges. In worship, service, and fellowship, we encounter Christ and are strengthened to carry His love into our homes, workplaces, and community. At St. James Catholic Church, you are invited not only to belong, but to actively share in our mission of discipleship and compassionate outreach.
