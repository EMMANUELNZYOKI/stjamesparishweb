Mass & Sacraments at St. James Catholic Church

At St. James, the celebration of the Holy Mass and the Sacraments is at the heart of our community life. Through these sacred moments, we encounter Christ, receive His grace, and are strengthened to live out our faith in the world.

  • Holy Mass: Join us for daily and Sunday celebrations of the Eucharist, the source and summit of our Catholic faith.
  • Baptism & Confirmation: Receive the gift of new life in Christ and the strength of the Holy Spirit.
  • Marriage & Reconciliation: Celebrate the beauty of Christian marriage and the healing mercy of God through confession.

Whether you are seeking to begin your journey of faith, return after time away, or simply deepen your relationship with Jesus, our parish family welcomes you with open arms and hearts.

For Mass times, sacramental preparation, or to learn more about any of our ministries, please contact the parish office or visit our Contact Page. We look forward to sharing these holy celebrations with you.
